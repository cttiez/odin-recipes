Sushi

Description

The above photo displays a mouth-watering presentation of my favorite dish: sushi. In this photo, there are a few standard maki's with a beautfully orange colored salmon sashimi and nigiri. The green paste next to the salmon is wasabi, which can be very spicy. The pink stuff next to it is ginger, and its purpose is to cleanse your taste buds between each bite.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Crab meat
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Salmon (Sashimi Grade)
  • Roasted Seaweed
  • Wasabi
  • Ginger
  • Masago (Fish Roe)

Steps

To make the crabmeat roll:

  1. Cook sushi rice until sticky.
  2. Using a bamboo mat, place seaweed on and apply a small layer of sushi rice to cover seaweed.
  3. Sprinkle masago all over the rice and flip entire seaweed over.
  4. Add cucumber, avocado, and crabmeat along the center length of the seaweed.
  5. With the bamboo mat, roll the seaweed with the contents and form into a square.
  6. Firmly press on all sides.
  7. Carefully cut into even pieces.
  8. Enjoy!

To make the salmon nigiri:

  1. Take sushi rice and mold into a rectangle, roughly the size of your thumb.
  2. Apply light soy sauce and wasabi on underside of salmon sashimi.
  3. Place the salmon on previously molded rice (soysauce and wasabi side down on rice.)
  4. Enjoy!

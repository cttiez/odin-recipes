Burger

Description

Build your own tasty burger at home! Burgers are a great option for throwing on the grill on a beautiful summer day. Friends and family can build their dream burger with ease, selecting their preferred condiments, veggies, and cheese. Patties only take about 7-10 minutes depending on the desired rarity of the meat. Pair this with your favorite beverage for an enjoyable meal!

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • All natural burger beef patties (my preference is 80% lean and 20% fat)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Cheese:
    • Pepperjack (my favorite!)
    • Cheddar
    • Pretty much anything you want, its your burger!
  • Condiments:
    • Ketchup
    • Mayonnaise
    • Hot sauce
    • Barbecue sauce
    • WHATEVER YOU WANT!

Steps

  1. Throw your choice of patties onto the grill.
  2. Depending rarity:
    • Well done: 7 minutes both sides
    • Medium well: 6 minnutes both sides
    • Medium: 5 minnutes both sides
    • Rare: 4 minutes both sides
    • ????: Just walk it by the grill
  3. After the first flip, place cheese on top of patty.
  4. Close the lid for remainder of cooking time (based on rarity)
  5. Begin constructing the burger after time is up.
    1. Grab bottom bun and place on plate
    2. Place patty on bottom bun.
    3. Add onions
    4. Add pickles
    5. Add tomatoes
    6. Add lettuce
    7. Add condiments on top of bun
  6. Place the top bun on top of your masterpiece and enjoy!

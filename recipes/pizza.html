Pizza

Description

Tired of order a pepperoni pizza only to find that they were conservative about the pepperoni? Don't worry, pizza is very easy to make at home and you can add as much pepperoni as your heart desires! If you are like me, you can throw on some pineapples as well and noone can stop you!

Ingredients

  • Premade pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded cheese - options are to your preference:
    • Four cheese blend
    • Cheddar
    • Mozarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Optional: Pineapples

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Take baking tray and place parchment paper to cover tray.
  3. Start with pizza sauce and cover center, leaving about 1 inch from edges.
  4. Apply choice of cheese to cover areas that are covered in pizza sauce.
  5. Apply toppings of pepperoni and other choices.
  6. Place pizza into the oven for 13 minutes at 450 degrees fahrenheit.
  7. Take out pizza after timer is done while being extra careful since it will be extremely hot.
  8. Enjoy!
